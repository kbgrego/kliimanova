# Project: HVAC Business Website with Future CRM

## Goal

Develop a modern web platform for an HVAC company that installs and services:

- Air conditioning
- Heating systems
- Heat pumps
- Ventilation
- Smart home climate automation

The platform should initially serve as a marketing website while being designed to evolve into a complete CRM without major architectural changes.

---

# Phase 1 - Marketing Website (MVP)

## Objectives

- Present the company professionally.
- Showcase completed projects.
- Increase customer trust.
- Generate leads.
- Be mobile friendly.
- Be SEO optimized.
- Support multiple languages (Ukrainian first, English later).

# Success Criteria

A visitor should be able to:

- Understand the company's services in under 30 seconds.
- Easily find examples of completed work.
- Submit a service request in under 2 minutes.
- Contact the company from any page.

An administrator should be able to:

- Manage all website content.
- Publish new projects.
- Respond to customer requests.
- Prepare for future CRM functionality without redesigning the system.
